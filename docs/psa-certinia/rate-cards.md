# Rate Cards

Rate Cards define the billing rates for resources on a project. A Rate Card has Rate Card Lines, each specifying a rate for a combination of Role, Region, or Resource.

## Rate Periods
A Rate Card can have multiple Rate Periods — date-bounded segments with their own rates. When a Rate Period ends, PSA uses the next active period's rates for new timecards.

## Rate Period Extension
Extending a Rate Period pushes its end date out without creating a new period — useful when negotiations are delayed.
