# Revenue Recognition

Governed by **ASC 606** (US GAAP) and **IFRS 15** (international). Revenue is recognised when — and only when — the performance obligation is satisfied (service/product delivered).

## Not the same as billing or cash collection

| Event | What it is |
|-------|------------|
| Billing | Invoicing the customer |
| Cash collection | Money actually received (independent timing) |
| Revenue recognition | When it's recorded as earned in the income statement |

## Recognition methods

- **Monthly** — SaaS subscriptions: recognise 1/12 of annual contract each month
- **Milestone** — recognise when specific deliverables are accepted
- **Percentage of completion** — for long-running projects, recognise proportional to work done
- **Upfront** — for one-time perpetual licences or non-refundable setup fees
